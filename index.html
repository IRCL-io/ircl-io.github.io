IDAHO ROBOT COMBAT LEAGUE

Welcome to the Idaho Robot Combat League! We are a 501(c)3 community non-profit with a mission to produce educational workshops and competitions to foster a community of combat robotics in Idaho

How We Help You Build Better Bots

Whether it's your first build, or your 87th, IRCL is a community in need of dedicated innovators with a passion for robot carnage. Do you want to build your own mechanical terror? IRCL has a vast list of the tools, parts, kits, and knowlege that you need to create your craziest designs.

Ever since I was a young child, I remember the fleeting instances of seeing robotics fighting on TV. Immediately I craved that experience, but it wasn't for a long time that I felt like building a cool robot like that was even possible,even when I became an engineer as an adult. Today however, because of the latest greatest technologies available like RC, drones, and 3D printing, it is easier than ever to make and maintain your awesome design. Our league helps with fostering that access and uniting people to make these experiences real for everyone.
- David Ultis, Founder

Our Mission

Why do we do this? Because we can!

Building robots and crashing them together for sport and with your friends is a pastime that continuially rewards roboteers of all ages and walks of life. From design and construction, to safety and compeition itself, there is no greater playing field to explore STEM concepts, and make exciting experiments collide.

Idaho Robot Combat League is a 501(c) 3 community non-profit that produces educational workshops and competitive events.

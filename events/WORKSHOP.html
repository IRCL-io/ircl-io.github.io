Robot Build Workshop

Join us for an unforgettable Combat Robot Building Workshop! From sparks to strategy, this workshop gives you everything you need to dive fully into the art and science of real combat robotics. Whether you're a curious beginner, a maker at heart, or a family looking for an amazing STEM activity, this event is built for you.

Idaho Robot Combat League | 1-day event

🔧 What You'll Build

These robots come from customized competitive kits provided at cost by BoomBox Robotics, crafted by local competitors and talented designers, with guidance from IRCL volunteers.

🤖 What's Included

Complete combat robot kit for your chosen design

+ Transmitter and controls

+ All necessary tools, components, and expert instruction

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Team-Friendly Format

Build as a team of up to 4 people - perfect for families, youth groups, friends, or parent-child teams. Everyone gets to participate in the building and strategy.

Come build, learn, tinker, and experience the thrill of combat robotics in an accessible, collaborative, and inspiring environment.

Family-friendly teams (up to 4 people) build real, battle-ready robots using custom kits from BoomBox Robotics, with help from IRCL volunteers. No experience needed.
